Registration & Winner Prediction
  1. Each Player to register on this site.
    Once you register, you have to play entire tournament.
  2. During registration, player needs to predict Winner of World Cup.
  3. An automatic bet for Rs.500 will be placed for your chosen team, which will be settled at end of World Cup.
Betting Process
  1. You can place bet for a match from Matches Tab or Dashboard tab.
  2. Cutoff time for Bet placement for any match is 30 minutes prior the start of match.
    • For matches starting at 3:30 PM IST, bet should be placed before 3:00 PM IST.
    • For matches starting at 7:30 PM IST, bet should be placed before 7:00 PM IST.
  3. All bets for the other players, will be visible only after cutoff has passed.
  4. However, you will be able to modify your bet up until start of the match (3:30 or 7:30) respectively.
  5. But to change your bet, you will have to double the amount of previous bet.
    • For e.g if for IND vs PAK match your original bet was Rs.50 for IND, you can change this bet up until the match start time (3:30 or 7:30) , provided your new bet amount is atleast Rs.100.
  6. The cutoff times for bet placement will not change even if the match is delayed due to any circumstances.
    Plz Refer Point-2 for cutoff
    No leniency to be given to defaulters in any case.
  7. Minimum bet amount for Super 12 is Rs.50.
    Minimum bet amount for Semi Final is Rs.100.
    Minimum bet amount for Final is Rs.200.
  8. The upper limit for bet amount is minimum bet for match * 6.
  9. If you fail to place a bet before the cutoff for any match, amount equivalent to minumum bet for the match will deducted from your account.
  10. There is no restriction on advance bet placements. You can bet for all 30 Super 12 matches in advance.
Settlement Process
  1. Settlement for every match will calculated based on below formula.
  2. If you win a particular bet, you will receive (Your bet amount / Total bet on winning team) x Total bet on losing team.
  3. Any defaulter for a match, will be accounted as bet on losing team.
  4. Even in a case where the match is abandoned or their are no actual winners in a match, if there are defaulters , they will be treated as losing bets and the money will distributed to all non-defaulters as per the formula in Point-1.
    • For e.g if in IND vs PAK, there are defaulters and all non-defaulters have bet on PAK, even if IND wins the match, total defaulted money will be distributed according to applicable ratio to players who bet on PAK.
    • In other case, if there are defaulters and match is abandoned, total defaulted money will be distributed according to applicable ratio to players who are non-defaulters.
  5. For WC Final, if there is no player, who correctly predicted the winner (during registration), the bet for Rs.500 will be null and void.
